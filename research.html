Imaging

Where it begins

Human engagement is essential in imaging because imaging is, at its most basic level, a communication instrument. Imaging is a language in the widest sense. Imaging is the language of space. Imaging is the most effective way of depicting and conveying spatial information from one person to another. The importance of imaging as a communication technique is what maintains humans at the centre of our debate. A picture is worth a thousand words.

Where it ends

Medical imaging technological breakthroughs over the last century have offered unparalleled prospects for noninvasive diagnostics and have established medical imaging as a vital aspect of today's healthcare systems. The multidisciplinary field of medical image analysis is one of the key areas of innovation that represents these achievements. Curiosity begins as an act of tearing to pieces or analysis.
